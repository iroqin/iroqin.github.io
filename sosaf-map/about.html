CLIMATE–FISCAL READINESS

Public assets need a climate-adjusted value.

This prototype explores whether Indonesia's fiscal authority is institutionally and analytically prepared to incorporate climate-change risk into the management of state-owned assets.

Explore the map
“Is Indonesia’s fiscal authority ready to incorporate climate-change risk into the parameters used to manage public finance and state-owned assets?”
Study geographyNational, initially focused on Semarang and Padang
Asset scopeProperty and immovable infrastructure
Climate hazardsSea-level rise, coastal flooding, and forest fire
Time horizonsPresent, 2030, and 2050
Target usersAcademia, DJKN, ministries, and policymakers
Potential applicationValuation, adaptation, and BMN insurance
All current asset records and hazard surfaces are synthetic and intended solely for research prototyping.
